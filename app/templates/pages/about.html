What is this?

Simplenickels is a simple implementation of fboil (GitHub), a flask boilerplate that's an extension of flask-boilerplate.

Really the only thing you can do here is send virtual nickels to other users.

fboil features the following:

  • User accounts
  • Confirmation email system
  • Password reset system
  • Account related decorators
  • Good testing coverage
  • Heroku-ready, Travis CI-ready, Twitter OAuth-ready
